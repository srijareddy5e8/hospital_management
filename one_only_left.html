The One Only Left

Author : Riley Sager

About the author : Riley Sager is the New York Times bestselling author of seven novels, most recently The Only One Left and The House Across the Lake. A native of Pennsylvania, he now lives in Princeton, New Jersey.

About the book : a young caregiver assigned to work for a woman accused of a Lizzie Borden-like massacre decades earlier. Now reduced to a schoolyard chant, the Hope family murders shocked the Maine coast one bloody night in 1929.
